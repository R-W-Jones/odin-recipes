Teriyaki Salmon

Description

Teriyaki salmon is a simple, flavorful dish where salmon fillets are glazed with a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce made from soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. The fish is usually pan-seared or grilled, giving it a slightly caramelized, sticky coating while keeping the inside tender and juicy. Served with rice and vegetables, it's a light yet satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Broccoli
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, mirin, and sugar in a pan.
  2. Pan-sear or grill salmon, brushing with the teriyaki sauce until glazed.
  3. Plate the salmon with rice and vegetables, drizzling extra sauce on top.
